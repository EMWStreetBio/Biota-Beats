Chucky Kim

Chucky Kim is a producer, thinker, and connector. From consulting brands towards social impact to writing hit songs, Chucky offers a unique approach to creative expression: create a world where it is easier to be good. As a graduate of Harvard Divinity School, he has written extensively on community activation, the nature of creativity, and the ethics of compassion. His great love, however, is producing music, with his most recent production, “Home:Word,” hitting number 2 on iTunes Japan. He believes that the purpose of art is simply to bring people together.
